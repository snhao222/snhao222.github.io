$\circledcirc$ Layer-level Latency Predictor Design
$\multimap$ Construct inference latency model of convolution operator based on abundant real experimental data. $\multimap$ Construct inference models of other operators. The model can predict the inference latency of operators usually used in CNN, including Pooling, Fully connected, Batch normalization, and activation functions.
